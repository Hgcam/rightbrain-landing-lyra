Acceptable Use Policy

This Acceptable Use Policy applies to anyone using our services, whether via our web application or our API.

Last updated 8 July 2024
On this page
1. Obey the law and act ethically2. Understand your use case and be careful with new technology3. Respect our infrastructure and those of our partners

If you are aware of any violations of this policy, or if you discover any inaccuracy, bias, or other harm in our services, please notify us immediately at trustandsafety@rightbrain.ai.

If we reasonably suspect that you - or someone using your customer account - has breached this policy, we may suspend or terminate your account in accordance with our Customer Terms.

1. Obey the law and act ethically

You must not use our services to access, store, distribute, create, or transmit any material that:

  • is unlawful, harmful, threatening, defamatory, obscene, infringing, harassing, or racially or ethnically offensive;
  • facilitates illegal activity, including fraud, phishing, or unauthorised access to systems or information;
  • depicts sexually explicit images;
  • promotes unlawful violence;
  • is discriminatory based on race, gender, colour, religion, sexual orientation, or disability;
  • is otherwise illegal or causes injury to any person or property.

2. Understand your use case and be careful with new technology

Large Language Models (LLMs) are powerful, but still experimental. They may produce inaccurate or offensive content.

Use discretion before relying on, publishing, or deploying LLM outputs.

If your use could have legal or significant effects on individuals:

  • ensure all LLM output is subject to human review;
  • and disclose the use of LLMs to the affected individuals.

Do not use our services for business-critical functions without assessing the risks of using LLMs.

Note

Our free-tier services are intended for testing and experimentation only. Do not:

  • use them for business-critical purposes;
  • or submit sensitive, confidential, or personal data.

3. Respect our infrastructure and those of our partners

You must not:

  • place excessive load on Rightbrain infrastructure;
  • copy, modify, or create derivative works of our services;
  • circumvent bans or account suspensions;
  • reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract source code.

If you use a third-party service like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini through our platform, you must also comply with their Acceptable Use Policies.

Spotted a violation?

Report misuse, inaccuracies, or other harm to our trust and safety team.
