Placement

Clear space & minimum size

Give the logo room to breathe and never render it below its minimum size, or legibility breaks down.

Clear space
1× icon height

Keep padding equal to the height of the icon clear on all sides. Nothing should intrude into that zone.

Minimum size · icon
24 × 24 px

Never display the icon smaller than 24px in digital contexts.

Minimum size · full logo
120 × 36 px

Below this the wordmark becomes hard to read. Use the icon instead.

Usage

Do & don't

The logo is a fixed asset. Any change beyond scaling needs sign-off from the brand team.

Do

  • Keep at least 1× the logo height of clear space around it.
  • Pick the variant that suits the background — full-colour on light, reverse on dark.
  • Preserve the aspect ratio when scaling.
  • Use vector (SVG) for digital wherever possible.

Don't

  • Distort, rotate or change the logo's proportions.
  • Add shadows, gradients or other effects.
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
  • Recolour the logo outside the approved variants, or use low-resolution files.
For developers

Design tokens

Drop these CSS custom properties in to match the product surface exactly. Values are in the oklch colour space with hex shown for reference.

tokens.css 
:root {
  /* accent */
  --rb-orange:      #EA580C;   /* oklch(0.646 0.222 41.116) */
  /* neutrals */
  --rb-ink:         #1C1917;   /* foreground / dark bg */
  --rb-paper:       #FFFFFF;   /* light bg / on-dark text */
  --rb-stone:       #78716C;   /* muted text */
  --rb-line:        #E7E5E4;   /* borders */
  /* type */
  --rb-font-head:   "Merriweather", Georgia, serif;
  --rb-font-mono:   "JetBrains Mono", ui-monospace, monospace;
  --rb-radius:      0;
}
Press

About Rightbrain

Boilerplate copy and key facts for stories and listings. Please use our name as “Rightbrain” (one word, capital R).

Rightbrain is a managed AI-agent platform that runs agents inside a company's core systems and workflows. Teams build reliable agents that follow their business rules, plug into existing tools, and execute tasks around the clock — then operate them at scale with full traceability.

Rightbrain offers agents as a service and as a platform: teams can build themselves, have Rightbrain build alongside them, or have Rightbrain run everything end to end. The company is based in the United Kingdom and raised a £3M seed round led by NPIF II in 2026.
